You've been asked for feedback?

Fewer than 1 in 10 freelancers are ever asked how their work experience could be improved. That’s not good enough.

One of your clients is trying to change that – by listening.

They’ve partnered with us at The Independency Co. to run an independent, anonymous survey of their freelancer community – including you.

By taking part, you’ll help your client understand what it’s really like to work with them – what works, what doesn’t, and where they can do better. Your insights feed directly into a confidential report we create for them, helping shape better ways of working for freelancers like you.

We're not just looking for positive feedback – we want to know what’s not working, and what they can do to improve. Your honest answers will help them make things better for everyone.

What do you do with my responses?

We'll gather your response, along with all of the other freelancers this business works with, anonymise the data, aggregate the results, and create a report.

This will show them where they're doing well, and where they can improve. It will also aim to show any money they're losing by not doing a good job. And showing a commercial loss usually encourages businesses to make improvements.

When you've completed the survey, we keep the anonymised data for 18 months, so if the client runs feedback again, we can compare the results.

But your contact details are not stored as part of this, so there's no way for us to email or contact you.

If its anonymous, why do you ask for my email address?

We need to verify that you are a member of this client's freelancer community. This client has provided us with a 'hashed' list of email addresses, which we compare your email address to.

We delete your email address from our database four weeks after the survey closes, and the client will never be able to see a connection between your responses and your email address.

What if I'm not happy to take part?

There's no obligation to take part. It won't affect your work or relationship with this client.

But we would encourage you to take part, as it will help the client improve their relationship with you, and the experience you have of working with them.

If you'd prefer to share your feedback via other means, you can email us directly at hello@independency.co

What is The Independency Co?

We're an organisation with a mission to create more confidence in freelancing.

We run projects like Leapers (supporting the mental health of freelancers), and freelancing.support (a free resource for freelancers).

We also run communities like Outside Perspective, and are building tools to help hirers and freelancers work together better.

Founded by Matthew Knight, Chief Freelancing Officer at The Independency Co., YunoJuno freelancer of the year, and award winning community builder, Matthew's work has supported over 250,000 freelancers.
