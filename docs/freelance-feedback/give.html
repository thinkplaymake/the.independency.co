The Independency Co. has built a platform to support freelancers to be heard by their clients.

Leave your feedback, and we'll connect with your client, to let them know freelancers want to share their thoughts.

It's entirely anonymous, so your relationship with your client is protected.

We'll invite them to run a full feedback process, to gather insights from all of their freelancers.

If they're not interested in listening, we'll publish anonymised data via our community network, once there's enough feedback to aggregate useful insights.

How do we use your data?

When you complete the feedback form - we'll anonymise your feedback, and the client know there's feedback waiting to be shared.

If they choose to run a full feedback process, your feedback will be included in the report.

If we have enough feedback on the same client, we'll publish the aggregated scores via our community network, so others understand common issues or success with this client.

If they don't run a feedback process, we'll store your data for 12 months, and aggregate with any other feedback on the same client.

Read the full privacy policy here.

Why are you doing this?

The goal is to encourage clients to actively ask for feedback from their freelacers - so they can listen, learn and improve their ways of working with their partners.

Our belief is that clients will benefit from knowing where there are issues, especially if it means they're losing money or efficiencies.

And that's what will lead to improvements for freelancers, and better working relationships.
