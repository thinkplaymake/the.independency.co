Why?

Why run a FERRI audit?

Most organisations don’t know how freelancers actually experience working with them.
As a result, they lose great talent, damage their brand, and waste money fixing it later.

An annual pool check lets you:

  • Spot and fix issues early
  • Improve rebooking rates
  • Protect your freelancer reputation
  • Strengthen your freelancer ROI and commercial performance

Why not ask them yourself?

Because they won’t tell you the truth.

As an independent third party - we are able to gather impartial and anonymous insights from your freelancers, so they can be honest without fear of judgement.

And we’re building a benchmark across many hirers, so you can see how you’re doing within your industry.
