Thanks for your feedback!

What happens next?

We'll reach out to the client you've provided feedback on, to let them know their freelancers are keen to share.

We'll highlight any key points we've heard from your feedback, as well as any feedback from other freelancers.

And we encourage them to run a full feedback process - where we work with the client to speak to their entire pool of freelancers.

We'll store your feedback for 12 months, and aggregate with any other feedback on the same client.

Once we have enough feedback on a client, we'll publish the aggregated scores via our community network - so if you're already a member of a freelancing community - ask if they're part of the Independency Community Coalition.

Our goal here is to get clients to take feedback and freelance experience seriously - and most importantly to make improvements.

It's going to take some time, but we're committed to supporting you as a freelancer, and that means tackling the client-side challenges.

If you've got any questions, drop me a note at hello@independency.co.

If you'd like to access ongoing support and resources, consider subscribing to freelancing.support, our platform for freelancers.
