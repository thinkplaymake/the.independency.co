href="https://unsplash.com">Unsplash</a></figcaption></figure></div><p><strong>For World Mental Health Day next month, the Independency Community Coalition is running its first shared webinar.</strong></p><p>Hosted by Matthew Knight of Leapers, the free session will be covering the key challenges and issues facing the wellbeing of the self-employed, and then looking at tangible techniques for working well when working for yourself.</p><p>The session will run on October 10th at 11am - to support World Mental Health day, and is free to all coalition community members. </p><p>Further information has been sharing with our coalition community spaces, along with registration details.</p><p>One of goals of the <a href="https://the.independency.co/community-coalition">Independency Community Coalition</a> is to reduce the cost, effort and complexity of hosting sessions like this, so more of our members can benefit from the insight and expertise across our spaces.</p><p>This is the first in a series of webinars, which will touch upon topics like IR35, Making Tax Digital, Dealing with Redundancy, and Pricing.</p><p>Independency Community Coalition members benefit from access to share resources, events, tools and insights, as well as a supportive network of fellow community founders.</p><p>The founding community members include Blkbk, Doing it for the Kids, We are She, Clear Connect Network, Elsie, Freelancing for Good, Freelancer Magazine, Leapers, Not your Business and Outside Perspective, and is welcoming new communities to apply to join the coalition.</p>
