# SB PM Network

SB PM Network is a community platform created exclusively for freelance and permanent advertising delivery professionals in the UK. In a short time, the network has become a trusted space where delivery experts can find new opportunities, get support with their search, engage in peer-to-peer learning, and connect with like-minded professionals virtually and in-person.
